# Discord Guide

## What is Discord?

Discord is a free app that communities use to talk, share, and learn
together. The Codetopia Community has its own space on Discord, called a
server, and that is where the community lives every day.

This guide will take you from zero to fully inside our server, step by
step. Take your time. There is no rush.

---

## Step 1: Download Discord and create your account

We recommend installing Discord on both your phone and your computer,
so you are never cut off from the community.

Discord has an official guide that walks you through this. Before you
open it, here is what to expect:

- You will download the Discord app (from your phone's app store, or
  from discord.com on a computer)
- You will create an account with a **username**, your **email address**,
  and a **password**
- Discord will send a **verification email**. Open your inbox and click
  the link inside it
- You do not need to change any other settings. Ignore everything else
  for now

<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/31676852332439-Discord-Sign-Up-and-Registration-Guide" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">👉 Discord Sign Up and Registration Guide</a>


**One important thing to know:** your Discord account is separate from
your Codetopia Community portal account. You now have two accounts. Keep both
email addresses and passwords somewhere safe. You will connect the two
accounts later in this guide.

You only need to create your Discord account once. If you install
Discord on a second device, just log in with the same email and
password. Everything follows you.

> **Once your account is created and verified, come back here and
> continue with Step 2.**

## Step 2: Turn on notifications

Turn on notifications on both your phone and your computer so you never
miss announcements, replies, or mentions from the community.

Discord's official guides show you where the setting is. You are looking
for one thing only: the setting that turns notifications **on**. Ignore
every other option for now.

<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/218892547--Mobile-Notifications-Settings-101" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">👉 Mobile notifications guide</a>
 
<a href="https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/215253258-Notifications-Settings-101" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">👉 Desktop notification guide</a>

> **Once notifications are on, come back here and continue with Step 3.**

## Step 3: How Discord works

Before you join our server, let's look at what you will see, so nothing
feels unfamiliar when you land. Discord looks different on a computer
and on a phone, so we will show you both.

### On a computer

On a computer, you see everything at once, in three areas side by side:

![Annotated full view of Discord on desktop, with colored shapes: yellow - server list, red - channels panel, white - message area](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/codetopiacommunity/community-howtos/main/images/Getting-Started/discord/discord-overview.png)

1. **The server list**, the strip of round icons on the far left. Each
   icon is a server, which is a community's own space. Click an icon to
   enter that server.
2. **The channels panel**, right next to the server list. A channel is a
   room for one topic. Channels are grouped into categories, like
   folders. Click a channel to open it.
3. **The message area**, the big space on the right. This is where the
   conversation happens. Type your message in the text box at the very
   bottom and press enter to send.

### On your phone

On your phone, you see one panel at a time instead of everything at once.

Discord mobile view showing the channel list with the server list strip on the left

![Discord mobile view showing the channel list with the server list strip on the left](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/codetopiacommunity/community-howtos/refs/heads/main/images/Getting-Started/discord/discord-mobile-channels.jpg)

Discord mobile view showing an open channel with the message area and text box
![Discord mobile view showing an open channel with the message area and text box](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/codetopiacommunity/community-howtos/refs/heads/main/images/Getting-Started/discord/discord-mobile-messages.jpg)

- The server list is the strip of round icons along the left edge
- Tap a channel name to open it. The conversation fills your whole screen
- **To go back to the channel list, swipe right.** Remember this one,
  it is the number one thing new members get stuck on

That is all you need to know. Now let's get you in.

## Step 5: Verify your account

Right now you can only see a few channels. The rest of the server is
locked until you verify. This is normal, and it takes two minutes.

When you enter the server, you will land in the **#welcome** channel,
where you will see a message like this:

> Welcome to Codetopia Community, @yourusername.
> Read #rules-and-guidelines, then head to #verify and run `/link` to
> connect your Codetopia portal account and unlock the server.

![The welcome channel showing the bot welcome message](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/codetopiacommunity/community-howtos/refs/heads/main/images/Getting-Started/discord/new-to-server.png)

Follow these steps exactly:

1. **Read the rules.** Open the **#rules-and-guidelines** channel and
   read through it. Every member is expected to know and follow the
   rules.

2. **Open the #verify channel.** Find **#verify** in the channels panel
   and open it.

3. **Type the link command.** Tap or click the message box at the bottom
   of the screen and type `/link` with no spaces. As soon as you type
   the `/`, a small menu will pop up above the message box showing the
   command. Select the **link** command in that menu, or press enter.

   ![The verify channel showing the /link command popup as it is typed](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/codetopiacommunity/community-howtos/refs/heads/main/images/Getting-Started/discord/run-link.png)

4. **Connect your portal account.** Follow what appears on screen. When
   you are asked to log in, use your **portal** email and password, not
   your Discord ones. This is the step that connects your two accounts.


5. **You are in.** Once your account is linked, all the channels unlock.
   Welcome to the Codetopia Community. 🎉

**If nothing happens when you type `/link`:** check that you are in the
#verify channel, and that there is no space before the `/`. If it still
does not work, ask for help right there in the channel. Someone will
help you.
