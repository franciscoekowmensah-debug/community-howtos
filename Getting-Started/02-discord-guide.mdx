# Discord Guide

## What is Discord?

Discord is a free app that communities use to talk, share, and learn
together. The Codetopia Community has its own space on Discord, called a
server, and that is where the community lives every day.

This guide will take you from zero to fully inside our server, step by
step. Take your time. There is no rush.

---

## Step 1: Download Discord and create your account

We recommend installing Discord on both your phone and your computer,
so you are never cut off from the community.

Discord has an official guide that walks you through this. Before you
open it, here is what to expect:

- You will download the Discord app (from your phone's app store, or
  from discord.com on a computer)
- You will create an account with a **username**, your **email address**,
  and a **password**
- Discord will send a **verification email**. Open your inbox and click
  the link inside it
- You do not need to change any other settings. Ignore everything else
  for now

👉 [Discord Sign Up and Registration Guide](https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/31676852332439-Discord-Sign-Up-and-Registration-Guide)

**One important thing to know:** your Discord account is separate from
your Codetopia Community portal account. You now have two accounts. Keep both
email addresses and passwords somewhere safe. You will connect the two
accounts later in this guide.

You only need to create your Discord account once. If you install
Discord on a second device, just log in with the same email and
password. Everything follows you.

> **Once your account is created and verified, come back here and
> continue with Step 2.**
---

## Step 2: Turn on notifications

Turn on notifications on both your phone and your computer so you never
miss announcements, replies, or mentions from the community.

Discord's official guides show you where the setting is. You are looking
for one thing only: the setting that turns notifications **on**. Ignore
every other option for now.

👉 [Mobile notifications guide](https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/218892547--Mobile-Notifications-Settings-101)
👉 [Desktop notifications guide](https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/215253258-Notifications-Settings-101)

> **Once notifications are on, come back here and continue with Step 3.**
