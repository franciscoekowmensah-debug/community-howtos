# Join the Codetopia Community

Welcome! We are glad you are here. The Codetopia Community is a space for
builders, learners, and creators figuring it out together, and this guide
will get you in step by step.

Joining happens in two parts:

1. **Create your portal account**, which gives you your identity in the
   community (this page covers it)
2. **Join our Discord server**, which is where the community lives and
   talks every day (the next guide covers it)

Let's start with the first one.

---

## What is the portal?

The portal is the Codetopia Community's home base. It is a website where
every member has an account. Your portal account is your identity in the
community. It is how we know who you are, and it is what unlocks
everything else, including access to our Discord server.

Think of it like this: the portal account is your membership card. You
create it once, and it opens every door in the community.

---

## Before you start

You will need two things:

- A working **email address** that you can open and read right now. You
  will receive a verification email during signup, so make sure you can
  access your inbox.
- Your **correct personal information**. Use your real name and real
  details. Members who sign up with information that does not belong to
  them will have their accounts removed or banned without notice.

---

## Create your account

1. Open your browser and go to
   [portal.community.codetopia.org](https://portal.community.codetopia.org)
2. Follow the steps on the screen to create your account. Read everything
   carefully and take your time. There is no rush.
3. The portal will guide you through a short onboarding, a few screens
   that introduce you to the community and ask for the information we
   need. Fill everything in correctly and completely.

**You will know you are done** when you can log in to the portal and see
your own account.

---

## What's next?

Your portal account is ready. Now it is time to join the community on
Discord, where all the conversations, learning, and collaboration happen.

👉 [Discord guide](https://community.codetopia.org/howtos/Getting-Started/02-discord-guide)
